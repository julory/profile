Educational Background Years 🏫

From 2018 to 2020, I worked as the Convener of Korea Youth English Debate Tournaments as an intern at DebateForAll and YTN Media Business Center. As a tournament director of ten tournaments, the hands-on experiences and endless passion drove me here. As a Junior, I served as the secretariat of the Model European Union and organized the entire conference in collaboration with the Delegation of the EU to the Republic of Korea and Access Communications. As a debater representing South Korea, our team received grand prize at the Debate Contest of Silk-road Universities Network held in University of Bucharest, Romania. As a senior, I have continued my career as a Tournament Director at YTN. I was selected as the Top 5 Finalist of the CEO For 1 Month Program at Adecco Korea, a Global HR Firm.
